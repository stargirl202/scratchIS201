I had the opportunity to serve a full time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from May 2022- November 2023. It was by far the hardest but most rewarding time of my whole entire life. I was able to grow closer to my Savior Jesus Christ and help others do the same. I learned so many valuable things on my mission.

    • To rely on Jesus Christ because he can do all things
    • Miracles can and will happen
    • It is the Lords work
    • Trust in Gods timing
    • His plan is perfect
    • Church history
  2. Life Skills
